ABOUT ME!

From managing gaming servers in high school to developing my own projects today, my journey has always revolved around games. I’m now a senior Digital Game Design student at Istanbul Aydın University, focusing on creating engaging gameplay systems and immersive experiences. Over the years, I’ve participated in multiple game jams, led small teams, and built several prototypes — each one teaching me how to design, code, and think critically under pressure. Alongside my technical work, I’ve improved my English to an academic level to collaborate internationally and follow industry standards. My goal is to continue developing games that combine strong mechanics with meaningful storytelling, and to keep learning as I move closer to becoming a professional game developer.
