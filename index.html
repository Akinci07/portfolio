ABOUT ME!

Even during high school, despite my academic responsibilities, gaming remained an integral part of my life. I not only played but also managed servers, which allowed me to develop a deeper understanding of game mechanics and community engagement. After successfully passing the university entrance exam, I was admitted to Istanbul Aydın University’s Digital Games and Design program. During my two years at university, I actively participated in various game jams and industry events, continuously refining my skills in game design and development. These experiences have strengthened my creative and technical abilities, further fueling my passion for interactive media and visual storytelling.

Concurrently, I elevated my English proficiency to an academic level, recognizing its importance in the global gaming industry. Throughout this journey, I found immense pleasure in pursuing my passion, realizing that I was taking significant steps towards my dream of becoming a game developer. Now, I am focused on achieving my future goals with unwavering belief in my abilities. Thank you.
