ABOUT ME!

Since childhood, I've always been curious about games. Initially, I would create intricate scenarios with my toys, immersing myself in my own imaginative world. As I grew older, my fascination shifted towards computer games. Minecraft, like many kids, became my favorite. I'd play on servers with friends, forming clans and engaging in epic battles while constructing massive fortresses. Throughout my school years, I explored a variety of computer games, each providing a unique experience. Later, my perspective on the gaming industry evolved with the introduction of a PS4 console, thanks to my family. I began to approach gaming from a different angle, closely following the market, examining newly released titles, and engaging in discussions with friends I made through the PS4. Our conversations extended beyond mere gameplay, delving into the intricacies of game design and development. Even during my high school years, amidst academic responsibilities, gaming remained a significant part of my life. I managed servers and indulged in gameplay, although my primary focus was on my studies. Upon passing the university entrance exam, I was admitted to Istanbul Aydin University's Digital Games and Design program. During my two years at university, I actively participated in various events and game jams, honing my skills and expanding my knowledge. Concurrently, I elevated my English proficiency to an academic level, recognizing its importance in the global gaming industry. Throughout this journey, I found immense pleasure in pursuing my passion, realizing that I was taking significant steps towards my dream of becoming a game developer. Now, I am focused on achieving my future goals with unwavering belief in my abilities. Thank you.

SCENARIO

As a game designer, I am well aware that our job is not only about software development or modeling. That's why I have read many books on storytelling and conducted research on game flows. This process may seem insignificant, but it is the most crucial aspect of the job, in my opinion.

My Portfolio

Fisher

In the game "Fisher," players control a boat that navigates between the harbor and fishing spots throughout the game's map. After reaching a fishing spot, players can catch fish and then proceed to the harbor to sell the caught fish. Throughout the game, players are pursued by a constant threat of a whirlpool behind them, adding tension and challenge to the gameplay.
