  • LastZ

    I developed this game with a team, where I took the role of team leader. It was one of my first 3D projects, and completing it required a lot of effort and persistence. Through this experience, I learned how to maintain communication and coordination within a team during a long-term project. I also took responsibility for solving numerous bugs and technical challenges. As the project progressed, we continuously added new features and eventually transformed it into a parkour-style game..

  • In our game, you play as a zombie character trying to survive by escaping from humans. That was the original idea, but later we decided to transform it into a parkour-style experience. We designed mechanics such as climbing ladders, jumping, grabbing onto pipes, slowing down time, killing enemies, and consuming them to regain stamina and health. The project remained unfinished, but it is still open to further development. I can honestly say that after a while it became quite challenging for us to continue.
